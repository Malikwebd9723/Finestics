1. Getting Started

Welcome to VegTrade Vendor Manager. This guide will help you set up your account and start managing your business efficiently.

Setting Up Your Profile

To ensure smooth operations, complete your business profile with accurate details:

  • Business Info: Enter your official business name and registration number.
  • Contact Details: Add a valid phone number and email for order notifications.
  • Address: Set your primary pickup/warehouse address.

2. Managing Orders

Efficiently handle incoming orders from your customers.

Processing New Orders

When a new order arrives, you'll receive a notification. To process it:

  • Go to the "Orders" tab.
  • Review the items and quantities requested.
  • Click "Accept" to confirm availability or "Edit" to propose changes.

Updating Order Status

Keep your customers informed by updating the order status as it moves through processing, packing, and dispatch.

3. Deliveries

Manage your fleet, drivers, and delivery routes.

Assigning Drivers

You can assign specific orders to drivers or vans:

  • Navigate to the "Delivery" section.
  • Select pending orders.
  • Choose a driver from the list to assign the route.

4. Payments & Invoices

Track your earnings and manage financial records.

Invoices

Invoices are automatically generated for completed orders. You can download them as PDF files for your records or to send to customers.

Tracking Payments

Monitor which orders have been paid and which are outstanding in the "Payments" dashboard.

5. FAQ

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login screen and click "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset it.

Can I change my business address?

Yes, you can update your address in the "Settings" > "Profile" section.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your business and customer data. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

6. Troubleshooting

Experiencing issues? Try these common fixes:

  • App Crashing: Ensure you are using the latest version of the app. Try clearing the app cache.
  • Login Issues: Check your internet connection. Verify your credentials.
  • Notifications Not Working: Check your device settings to ensure notifications are enabled for VegTrade.
